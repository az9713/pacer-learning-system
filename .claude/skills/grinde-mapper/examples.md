## Tips for Creating Your Own

1. **Start messy** - First draft doesn't need perfect layout
2. **Chunk first** - Identify major sections before drawing
3. **Add connections last** - Easier to see relationships after chunking
4. **Use consistent symbols** - Develop your personal vocabulary
5. **Include the backbone** - Forces you to distill the essence
